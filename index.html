About Us

WHO WE ARE AND WHAT WE DO

Anshul Group is a leading conglomerate operating in the state of Bihar. We are a uniquely diversified group across various sectors with interests in Construction,Sports, Food & Beavrage and the social services sector.

Over the years , the Anshul group has positioned itself as a leading and modern player in the construction business focussing on high-rise residential construction with high level operational excellence and benchmarking ourselves with the leading construction companies in the country. For the last 8 years, we have contributed to the sustainability and growth of Bihar’s economy through our flagship company- Anshul Homes and diversifying into Sports, Food and the social sector to promote the youth of the state besides generating employment for the state and indirectly sustainable growth for the anciallry industries also.

Being environmentally conscious, we are putting in place a sustainable environment management plan in place to as to reduce our carbon footprint on the earth while ensuring development.
